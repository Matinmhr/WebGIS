Pollutant analyzed

PM2.5

Fine particles smaller than 2.5 micrometres that penetrate deep into the lungs and bloodstream, posing serious risks to respiratory and cardiovascular health.

PM10

Coarse particles smaller than 10 micrometres, mainly from dust and traffic. They irritate the airways and can worsen respiratory conditions like asthma.

NO2

A gas primarily emitted by vehicle exhausts and combustion. It contributes to smog formation and is linked to lung inflammation and reduced lung function.
